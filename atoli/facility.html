TREKKING AT IGATPURI

After a scrumptious breakfast, you can pick a trekking kit of your choice and opt for a soft, medium, or hard trek. Special trekking packages can be arranged as per need and the length of your stay. You can surely witness the real Maharashtra by trekking in the hills.

FUN FILLED ACTIVITIES

Our Resort offers complimentary access to indoor and outdoor games. You can opt for playing Box Cricket, Badminton, Volleyball etc. Special matches and team activities can be arranged for corporates as needed.

This friendly resort is a beautifully maintained place with a long facade of wood and glass leading onto a garden, terrace; the hill peaks seem within touching distance. A sister venture of Rainforest Resort.
